What does "Paid ✓" mean?

That person completed payment on Razorpay. If someone paid but shows "Not paid yet", tap "✓ They paid" after checking Razorpay.

How do I message many people at once?

My Students → "Send one message to many" → write your message once → tap WhatsApp next to each person. The message is pre-filled every time.

How do I create the webinar meeting link?

Plan the webinar, then follow the 3 numbered steps on its card. For Google Meet: a calendar page opens with everything filled in — click "Add Google Meet video conferencing", press Save, copy the meeting link, and paste it back in step 2.

Where do Instagram numbers come from?

You copy them from the Instagram app once a week (Professional dashboard → Insights). YouTube numbers appear automatically once Rahul switches that on.

Something looks wrong?

Tap "Lock", open again, and log in. If it still looks wrong — send Rahul a screenshot 🙂
