Playbook for coaches

  1. 3 posts/week: 1 tip reel, 1 student win / testimonial, 1 behind-the-scenes.
  2. Reels < 30s with a hook in the first 2 seconds ("Your child speaks well at home but freezes on stage?").
  3. Bio link → always the UTM-tagged isonali.com link.
  4. Story poll every week — polls double reach and tell you what parents want.
  5. Reply to every comment/DM within a day — DMs are where enrolments happen.
  6. Local hashtags: #shivamogga #publicspeakingindia #kidsactivities alongside niche ones.
